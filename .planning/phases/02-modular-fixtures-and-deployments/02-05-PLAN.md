## Phase Goal

**As a** show author, **I want to** validate fixtures and adapt logical pools to concrete deployments through atomic impact review, **so that** one show adapts to different rigs without being rebuilt.

**This plan's slice:** A show author can run `golc pool update <pool> --add <fixture> --remove <member> --out plan.json` to get a deterministic dry-run impact review (human-readable, or `--json`), then `golc pool apply plan.json --plan-id <id>` to apply it atomically — with integrity + freshness gates that reject a tampered or stale plan, and review-before-apply as the default.

<objective>
Build the pool impact-review engine and its atomic, revision-guarded apply. `internal/pool/impact.go` computes a deterministic `ImpactPlan` for a pool add/remove request by walking every dependent the show model currently contains (deployment instances and cross-pool groups) and auto-proposing next-free universe/address for new instances (D-11). `internal/pool/plan.go` binds the plan by `plan_id = sha256(canonical body)` and enforces the same two-gate contract as `internal/trace/apply/guard.go` — `ValidatePlanIntegrity` (self-hash recompute) then `ValidatePlanFreshness` (recompute from current ShowState and compare, keyed on the ShowState `Revision`, D-16) — before an all-or-nothing apply. `internal/command/pool.go` exposes the Terraform-style plan/apply split (D-15): `pool update` computes and writes/prints the plan and mutates nothing; a separate `pool apply` validates and applies. Propagation is configurable per update (POOL-04) with review-before-apply (preview) as the default.

**Phase-boundary note (not scope reduction):** POOL-03 enumerates dependents "groups, themes, palettes, scenes, chases, motion presets, and controller mappings." Themes/palettes/scenes/chases/motion presets/controller mappings are introduced in Phases 3 and 6 — they do not exist in the Phase 2 show model. The impact walk therefore reviews every dependent category that exists now (deployment instances, groups) and is structured as an extensible dependent-category traversal so Phase 3+ dependent types plug into the same walk without a rewrite. This is the correct phase boundary, not a simplified subset of an in-scope feature.

Purpose: POOL-03/04/05/08 are the "review-before-apply, atomic, revision-safe" contract at the center of the phase's value — adapting one show to different rigs safely.
Output: `internal/pool/{impact,plan}.go`, tests, and the `pool update` / `pool apply` routes.
</objective>
