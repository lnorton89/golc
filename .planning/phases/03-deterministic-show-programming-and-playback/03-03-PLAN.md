## Phase Goal

**As a** show author, **I want to** build reusable chases and motion presets, **so that** I can add tempo-driven movement and stepped looks to scenes without re-authoring them.

This slice (03-03) adds the two time-varying reusable objects that scene chase/motion layers (03-04) reference and the engine (03-07) evaluates against musical position.

<objective>
Deliver reusable chases with ordered, tempo-relative steps (PROG-05) and motion presets scoped to position/beam capabilities (PROG-06), persisted as new `Chases` and `MotionPresets` fields on `show.State`, driven by `chase create` / `motion create` routes.

Purpose: Chases and motion presets are the tempo-driven programming objects the scene chase and motion layers combine; their determinism (no randomization, one authoritative clock) is what makes SCEN-09 achievable downstream.
Output: `internal/programming/chase.go`, `internal/programming/motion.go`, extended `show.State`, `chase`/`motion` command routes.
</objective>
